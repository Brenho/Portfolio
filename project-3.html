Bill Hicks
FoundationFor Wildlife

Project Brief

The Bill Hicks Foundation For Wildlife is a non-profit organisation set up in the memory of the late comedian. Based in Austin, Texas they are passionate about rescuing and rehabilitating the native wildlife.

What I delivered

The charity previously had a website in place. Unfortunately it was outdated and required some love. I created an entire new site which would bring them into the present. Fully responsive and mobile friendly. Lots of fancy Jquery effects and a heavy focus on large imagery gives the charity a modern face.
