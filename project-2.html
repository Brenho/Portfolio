DrunkenMaster KSU

Project Brief

DrunkenMaster KSU is a bar located in Busan, South Korea. Aimed at primarily foreigners.
Owned by a dynamic half Korean, half American couple, they asked me if I could create a small site to highlight their food and cocktail menu.

What I delivered

I created a responsive, mobile-friendly, single-page website which attempted to capture the vibe and style of the bar. Incorporating parallax and auto-scroll effects, with a lightbox gallery elevated this from simply a one-page advert.
