ISS TRACK

Project Brief

I have always had a keen interest in space and astrology. While there are countless websites with information on the International Space Station, many of them are outdated. So I decided to create my own.

What I built

Using HTML/CSS, Jquery and two brilliant API's, I created a site which displays the location of the ISS. Updating every 3 seconds and displaying various measurements such as velocity, altitude and position.
Also available is the current crew members (https not currently supported by the open-notify api).
